Where did the idea of creating a little spoon came from ?

Basically, one of our ancesters notices that serve or use some food, their spoons were too big for instance to get to the bottom of the jar when they tried to eat jam for their breakfast, or that they kept breaking their soft-boiled eggs. So they decided to act on it

This is how the fisrt "Golden Spoon" little spoon was created
